Pattern Matching Problems

Dated: June 19, 2017

In a pattern matching problem we are given a long text T and a pattern P. We are supposed to find the number of locations of P in T and possibly report their positions. This problem can be made more specific with a few more restrictions and conditions, which we will discuss later. A lot of work has been done on this problem and we have many good solutions. Many of which I would like to discuss in the form of a series of papers as stated in the previous webpage.

I will discuss the problem in from the selected paper's point of view, so its more of a discuss of the paper, rather than the problem.But our selection of papers will lead us to a wholesome discussion of the topic.
